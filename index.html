EcoGenies Cleaning Services

Welcome to EcoGenies Cleaning Services Zimbabwe. We provide professional cleaning services in Zimbabwe. EcoGenies specialises in commercial and residential cleaning services. We use eco-friendly cleaning products which are non-toxic as our green cleaning solution. Our cleaning rates are affordable. All cleaning prices include detail-clean rotations and routine site inspections to ensure your peace of mind. We only hire the best and most experienced cleaners and offer our services to a broad range of businesses including offices, medical centres, sports and recreational centers. We also offer our services to residential homes.

Green Cleaning
EcoGenies specialises in green cleaning solution. Green cleaning is the process of choosing cleaning products that are better for both people and the planet.Switching to green cleaning products is good for the health and wellbeing of people. It also prevents unnecessary chemicals from being released into the environment. Our Green cleaning includes the use of bicarbonate of soda, microfibre, steam and many other natural cleaning products and environmentally friendly cleaning techniques.

Avoid ever needing a spring-clean again!

EcoGenies is a commercial cleaning service provider rather than a traditional cleaning agency. Our vision is to provide an all-inclusive cleaning solution at the best quality with minimum interruptions. Our Detail-Clean Rotation System supports this vision by dividing your premise into zones. Over the course of 4 to 5 weeks, all zones are detail cleaned. This thorough cleaning is equivalent to at least 10 spring-cleans every year. A scoring system is used in our quality control regular site inspections, in order to guarantee that the detail cleaning of your premises meets our quality standards. If a cleaning visit fails to pass our standards, we repeat it at no extra cost.
